How to Reorder PDF Pages

Examples:
  • 3,1,2,4,5 - Move page 3 to the beginning
  • 2,1,3,4,5 - Swap first two pages
  • 5,4,3,2,1 - Reverse all pages
Note: Make sure to include all page numbers. Missing pages will be excluded from the output.
